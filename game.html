Why play this game?

Ocean health is closely related to our health. To help save our biggest ecosystem and the innocent animals, we must make ocean-friendly choices. Amongst many things but by eliminating plastic waste and organizing beach cleanups.

More information can be found here.

What you will need

How to play:

Part 1

  1. Click the download button above to print the PDF and cut out the domino pieces
  2. Each player draws 5-7 pieces
  3. The shortest player begins by laying out a piece
  4. Match pictures with pictures as shown in the picture above
  5. If you cannot can't lay any pieces, draw in 1 pieces, if it does not match wait until the next round
  6. The person who finishes all their pieces first gets 10 points
  7. If no one is able to finish, the person with the least pieces left gets 5 points

Part 2

  1. Talk to an adult about going outside
  2. Put on clothes, gloves or other items according to the weather
  3. Bring trashbags and something to write on to keep score of your points
  4. Go to your nearest beach/sea (if you don't have one nearby, go in your local area)
  5. Gather all the trash you find, but be careful
  6. Bring all the trash back and ask an adult where to dispose it
  7. Add all your points to see who won!
Task Points
Help an animal 25 points
Bottles to recycle 5 points
Full trashbags 10 points per bag
Things that need Recycling station 20 points per item
