LIFE BELOW WATER

To conserve and sustainably use the world’s ocean, seas and marine resources.

Goal

Goal 14 is about preserving life under water. Therefor the goal of this page is to raise awareness of this problem, through a simple game of picture domino.

Learn more

Purpose

The ocean supports life on Earth and regulates the climate system. It is the largest ecosystem in the world and is home to approximately one million known species.

The purpose of raising awareness is to encourage children, especially targeting kids in primary education. Making them aware at a young age will hopefully give them a better understanding, and mindfulness of the situation.
